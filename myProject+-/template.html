HOW TO SEARCH FOR A BET

You can search for a bet by following the simple steps below;

  • Step 1: Log in to your account
  • Step 2: Click the 'My Account' icon on the top right corner of your screen
  • Step 3: Click on 'outcome' to filter by the status(Running, Lost, Won or All) of the bet
  • Step 4: Select 'Bet Search' on the left side of the page
  • Step 5: Filter the 'Date From' and 'Date To' to the period of the bet placed.
  • Step 6: Click on 'proceed' to display your bets.
